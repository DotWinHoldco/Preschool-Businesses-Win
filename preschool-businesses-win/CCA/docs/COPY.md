## ENROLL `/enroll` — PASTOR funnel

### Hero
- Eyebrow: ENROLLMENT
- H1: The preschool search ends here.
- Sub: Enrolling at CCA starts with a simple application. Four steps, about five minutes. We'll be in touch within two business days.
- CTA: Start your application ↓

### P — Problem
- Eyebrow: THE PROBLEM
- H2: Finding a preschool you trust shouldn't be this hard.
- Body: You've read the reviews. You've driven past the buildings. You've toured places that smelled like bleach and places that smelled like chaos. Some had beautiful websites and empty playgrounds. Others had great teachers but no communication — you dropped your child off and heard nothing until pickup.

  You don't need a preschool with a fancy lobby. You need one where the teachers know your child's name, your child's allergies, and your child's favorite book. You need to know what happened today — not in a two-word answer at pickup, but in a real report with photos and details. You need to trust the people in the room.

### A — Amplify
- Eyebrow: WHY IT MATTERS
- H2: These years don't come back.
- Body: The research is clear: the preschool years shape everything that follows — language, social skills, emotional regulation, curiosity, confidence. The wrong environment doesn't just waste tuition. It shapes your child's relationship with learning, with authority, with other kids, with themselves.

  And it shapes your peace of mind. Every parent deserves to go to work knowing — not hoping, knowing — that their child is safe, happy, and growing.

### S — Solution
- Eyebrow: THE CCA DIFFERENCE
- H2: A preschool built on what actually matters.
- Body: Crandall Christian Academy was founded on a simple idea: children thrive when they're known, loved, and guided with intention.

  Here's what that looks like in practice:

  - **Small ratios.** [X]:1 in every classroom. Your child is never lost in a crowd.
  - **Trained, certified staff.** Every teacher is CPR/First Aid certified, background-checked, and trained in early childhood development. Not babysitters — educators.
  - **Daily transparency.** You'll receive a detailed daily report — meals, naps, activities, mood, milestones, photos — pushed to your phone before you leave work.
  - **Faith-integrated curriculum.** Bible stories, chapel time, and character lessons woven naturally into every day. Age-appropriate. Welcoming to all families.
  - **Real security.** Controlled access doors. Authorized pickup enforcement. Health screening at every check-in. Allergy protocols that don't rely on memory.
  - **A director who answers the phone.** Not a call center. Not a "we'll get back to you." A real person who knows your family.
